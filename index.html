Why Join Us

Fight climate Change

Climate change won't just impact forest, or coral reefs, or even people in far-off countries it will affect all of us. From more extreme weather to increasing food prices, to recreation and decreased opportunities to appreciate the natural world, people everywhere will feel its effects. in order to fight climate change , we carry out activities such as :
Act against forest loss by planting trees Eat food that is local and in season by encouraging people to produce more and avoid importing goods Spread the word by Encouraging friends, family and co-workers to reduce their carbon pollution .

Promote African Products And Brands

The overall sustainability of the African economy amongst other factors depends not only on her capacity to locally produce / manufacture raw materials, semi-finished and finished goods. The impacts and benefits of consuming these products by African on the continent and in the diaspora are countless for the continent at various levels. No matter the level at which we collaborate in this course, we are contributing to a new narrative for Africans at the grassroots and the continent as a whole.In our little contributions however it may be, we are abetting poverty and promoting a friendly environmental local industrialization, creating jobs, Fighting african illegal migration, rural exodus and mitigating social deliquescence.

Friendly Volunteer

Career development
Networking Opportunities
A sense of community
