About us

Who we are

OAB aims to promote and encourage the consumption of African brands/ made and local products for the sustainability of livelihood from the grasshood level using technology.We Fight climate change by reaching out and collaborating with some of its key 'actors': the farmers and producers.

Mission

●Encourage the consumption of locally made products and African brands for growth and sustainability,
●Create visibility for the local producers for sustainable livelihood,
●Create a sustainable market space for local / African brands or made a cross our boarders,
●Educate the African local producers, manufacturers and farmers on the importance of climate change awareness and the role each, in their economic activities can play to fight it,
●Collaborate with all relevant legal bodies to ensure our objectives.

Vision

●To become one of the leading Afrocentric organizations leading the part for a sustainable African economy by creating sustainability of livelihood in the near future,
●To Create a sustainable scam-free e-trans Africa network for sourcing/ trade for the diaspora and the world at large
●Showcase and promote the innovative, creative industrialization and entrepreneurship among the growing African youths
●Ours is to assist write a narrative of a somewhat stable African economy emerging from the local communities entrepreneurship ecosystem.

Values

● Sustainability, accountability and humanity in service,
● We promote honesty, integrity, and transparency in all our dealings,
● We foster an environment of lasting collaboration,
● We believe that a Healthy environment/climate is an overall sustainability.
