About us

Who we are

Open Aid Bridge is an international development non governmental Afrocentric organization working in remote and less privileged communities in the Sahel region in Africa. Our charity assists local producers, promote and encourage locally made products and goods for consumption and sustainability of livelihoods. Tackle climate crisis by influencing their overall internal policies towards friendly environmental decisions.

What We Do

We engage with local manufacturers , producers and farmers in Africa. Our projects address issues around sustainability of livelihood and the effect of climate change by providing technical support, promoting what they produce and influencing their overall internal policies towards friendly environmental choices. Our projects ensure that local producers thrive so that they can provide food security, sustainable livelihoods and protect the environment for today and tomorrow.

MISSION

Encourage the consumption of locally made products and African brands for growth and sustainability,
Create visibility for the local producers for sustainable livelihood,
Create a sustainable market space for local / African brands or made a cross our boarders,
Educate the African local producers, manufacturers and farmers on the importance of climate change awareness and the role each, in their economic activities can play to fight it,
Collaborate with all relevant legal bodies to ensure our objectives.

VISION

To become one of the leading Afrocentric organizations leading the part for a sustainable African economy by creating sustainability of livelihood in the near future, To Create a sustainable scam-free e-trans Africa network for sourcing/ trade for the diaspora and the world at large, Showcase and promote the innovative, creative industrialization and entrepreneurship among the growing African youths

Ours is to assist write a narrative of a somewhat stable African economy emerging from the local communities, entrepreneurship ecosystem.

VALUES

Sustainability, accountability and humanity in service, We promote honesty, integrity, and transparency in all our dealings, We foster an environment of lasting collaboration, We believe that a Healthy environment/climate is an overall sustainability.

For the Sustainability of livelihood and a healthy climate..
