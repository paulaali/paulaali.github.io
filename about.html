About us

Who we are

OAB aims to promote and encourage the consumption of African brands/ made and local products for the sustainability of livelihood from the grass hood level using technology. Fight climate change by reaching out and collaborating with some of its key 'actors': the farmers and producers.

Mission

create a sustainable market space for local / African brands or made a cross our boarders Educate local producers and farmers on the importance of climate change awareness and the role each, in their economic activities can play to fight it. Collaborate with all relevant legal bodies to ensure our objectives .
Moving from the hinterlands to the cities to discover local producers or enterprises and assess their capacities.

Vision

To Create a sustainable scam-free e-trans Africa sourcing/trade even with the rest of the world. Showcase and promote innovative, creative industrialization and entrepreneurship among the growing African youths. Provide stress-free sourcing within and among African countries and the globe.

Values

Sustainability, innovation, creativity via an accountable, respectful, effective, and efficient service.
We promote honesty, integrity, and transparency in all our dealings. We foster an environment of lasting collaboration.
