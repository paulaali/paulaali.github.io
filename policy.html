Our Policy

Our Modus Operandi

In a fastly developing world governed by tech, Open Aid Bridge aims to promote and encourage the consumption of African brands and locally made products for sustainability of livelihood from the grass hood level using technology. Fight the climate change crisis by reaching out and collaborating with some of its key 'Players': the farmers and producers.

Our clients

In view of the aforementioned, Open Aid Bridge hopes to create an environment for partnerships, Synergies, funding opportunities and lasting tailored solutions.Hence we objectively: Showcase, Promote and Sale African products, Educate and collaborate with the African farmer, producer and manufacturers for sustainability and, Source for you.

Buyers / Customers

We take charity to another level. We are donating and investing into lives in Africa the right way. Buying / sourcing with Open Aid Bridge means amongst other things: Providing a stable income to homes, Sending a child to school, Providing mental rest to a mother, father, etc, Contributing to positive climate actions.
In the light of the scope of our aim and objectives, Open Aid Bridge is primarily a charity that supports local producers and uses her social media handles amongst others to promote African Products in ways she can. Open Aid Bridge is not an e-commerce company.

We operate three (3) membership packages to the profit of as much who are in need of the assistance we offer.
The pro-bono member : these are our primary focus. African producers, manufacturers and farmers in remote and less privileged African communities who are struggling with their products. This is wholly free.
The Classic Member : these are African producers who are somewhat making it. However, they would like to leverage taking advantage of our social media marketing or other services we provide.
The 3rd Membership is for a local entrepreneur who doesn't deal with local products however needs managerial support but can't afford it elsewhere. Membership 2 & 3 come with a commitment token of $10 yearly, to assist the organization run the website to the benefits of its members. Being a charity organization we depend on donations to serve.

Policy Disclaimer:

These policies may be amended or altered as need arises and as management deems fit for the overall good of both members and customers. We are subject to individual Countries laws. Therefore, be sure what you are ordering is allowed to enter the country you are ordering it from.
It is expected of members to be honest and act professional towards the customer we expose them to. Failure to do so will amount to their expulsion from all OAB platforms, programs and privileges as the case may be without prior notification. Customers are recommended to report any inconvenience or dissatisfaction as per the services received for address and improvements. Remember, we are in this together, for a better Africa!

All orders will be delivered within 7 working days or more depending on the time of the order and the location of the goods or customers. Open Aid Bridge has no storage of items promoted. We kindly request for your patience and understanding.
Every financial transaction between an OAB member and a customer should be effected through the organization 's bank account for the safety of both members trader and customers.
Money paid for a commodity shall be transferred into the member's account 24 hours after customer 's confirmation of receipt & satisfaction. Should the customer remain silent on any of these two counts, OAB shall rely on the delivery notice/report by the courier in charge of the delivery and pay into members' account.OAB and its members will therefore be free of any responsibility thereafter.

Customers are free to request to deal with the member trader directly and vice versa. In this case, OAB shall be exempted from any responsibility in case of any eventuality insuring from such transactions. Hence: Conversation/contract solely between member traders and customers are out of OAB jurisdiction.
Financial transactions directly into member trader accounts are out of OAB jurisdiction. Customers are expected to check the product upon receipt for possible replacement or any other insensitive form from the trader.
Replacement shall only be if product or item received do not correspond to that which was ordered for. Customers are required to report any dissatisfaction as per product quality immediately upon delivery for possible action.

We Do Not operate refund policies. All purchases with Us are considered as acts of charity. Kindly take note :
Member traders may also have their operating policies to be complied with. As a charity organization, we are here to create visibility and accessibility by operating as an assisting bridge to network the African Farmer, Manufacturers, products to Africa, the diaspora and the world in our little way.
